Call of Duty Black Ops 2 Overview







Released in November 12th of 2012, Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 is a first-person shooter game developed by Treyarch and published by Activison. Being the 2nd game of the Black Ops series, Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 remains a successful predecessor to it’s previous game Call of duty:Black Ops. In this game you have a well-built story mode campaign with lots of adrenaline rushing moments, and fascinating scenes, as well as loads of customizable weapons and maps in multiplayer. Call of Duty Black Ops 2 remained the biggest entertainment launch all the way until 2013. Pushing the boundaries of what fans have come to expect from the record-setting entertainment franchise, Call of Duty®: Black Ops II propels players into a near future, 21st Century Cold War, where technology and weapons have converged to create a new generation of warfare.
